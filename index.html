Recipe of Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

Marination:

  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • salt of taste
Rice:
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Spices (cumin,fennel,peppercorns,cardamon,cloves,bay leaf,cinnamon)
  • Red chilli powder, turmeric powder
  • Ghee/oil
  • Salt

Instructions:

Marination:
  1. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, and salt.
  2. Coat chicken, marinate 2+ hours or overnight.
Rice:
  1. Soak Basmati rice for 30 mins.
  2. Boil with spices and salt, drain
Layering:
  1. In a large pot, alternate layers of rice and chicken masala.
  2. Top with fried onions and herbs. Cook on low heat for 15-20 mins.

Enjoy your Chicken Biryani!
